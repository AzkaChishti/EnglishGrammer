Past Perfect Continuous

The past perfect continuous tense is used to describe an action which had been going on for some time and finished at some definite time in the past


Formula


Subject+had been+1 st form of verb+ing+object+since,for

Examples

*I had been waiting for you since 2 o'clock
*Boys had been making a noice for 2 hours
*Had she eating food for two days??
*Had they not eating food since 12 o'clock?
