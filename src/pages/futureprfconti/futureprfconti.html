Future Perfect Continuous

The Future perfect continuous tense is used to describe an action which has been going on continuously and as yet to be completed in the future


Formula


Subject+shall have been,will have been+1 st form of verb+ing+object+since,for

Examples

*I shall have been waiting for you for 2 days
*Boys will have been making a noice for 2 hours
*will she have been eating food for two days??
*will they have not been preparing for examination since 12 july
